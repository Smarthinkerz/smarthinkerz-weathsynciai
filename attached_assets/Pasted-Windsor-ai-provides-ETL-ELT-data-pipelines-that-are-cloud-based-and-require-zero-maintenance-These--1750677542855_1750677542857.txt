Windsor.ai provides ETL/ELT data pipelines that are cloud-based and require zero maintenance. These pipelines load all your data from sources such as Salesforce, HubSpot, Facebook Ads into Google BigQuery and update it regularly. By having all your data stored in a high-performance cloud warehouse, you can perform various tasks ranging from basic reporting to intricate ad-hoc queries. Additionally, it allows you to conveniently merge data between different sources. Windsor.ai connectors can be set up in just five minutes and can adapt to any changes in the source automatically. This means that your data engineering team can focus on creating value instead of spending time on adjustments.

Windsor.ai typically generates a fully configured and predetermined schema for most data sources. This ensures that you still have access to all the necessary data.

How can you quickly load your data and begin your free trial with Windsor.ai?

Step 1: Connect your data source  
Step 2: Click on the "Sign Up with Windsor.ai" button located above and follow the prompts.
Step 3: Create a new transfer and complete the transfer form.
Step 4: Wait for the initial historical transfer to complete syncing, which may take several minutes or even days depending on the size of your data source.
Step 5: Start utilizing your data.

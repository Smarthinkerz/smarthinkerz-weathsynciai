Replit Sprint plan: WealthSynci AI

User Skills & Capability Validation Mechanisms

Portfolio Upload & URL Showcase
Allow users to:
Upload past work (PDFs, reports, decks, financial models).
Link to external URLs (Notion, GitHub, personal websites, or financial project platforms).
Tag uploads by skill (e.g., "Investment Strategy," "ESG Research").
B. Reference and Endorsement Collection

Let users request and submit:
Professional references (with optional verification from company email).
Endorsements from verified contacts (similar to LinkedIn).
Star ratings and reviews from collaborators or clients.
C. Certifications & Credentials Upload

Allow upload of degrees, certifications (e.g., CFA, CFP), and badges.
Integrate with Certiport, Credly, or LinkedIn Learning to verify.
D. Client/Deal Feedback

If the user has done freelance or investment work on-platform:
Clients or companies rate them post-collaboration.
Results feed into a verified track record dashboard.
Company Capability Validation Mechanisms

A. Case Study or Portfolio Submission

Companies submit documented success cases:
Past deals, partnerships, market entries.
Supporting files like investment decks, whitepapers, or performance analytics.
AI tags and rates credibility of each item.
B. Official Website & Social URL Verification

Require verified domain email and matching website/social media.
Option to verify:
Company LinkedIn
Crunchbase
Public databases
C. Client Testimonials and Partner References

Submit testimonials from partners or clients with contact or company name.
Option for WealthSync to reach out or verify.
D. Third-Party Validation & Integration

Sync with platforms like:
G2, Trustpilot (for reviews)
Clutch (for project-based validation)
Salesforce/Xero (to pull verified activity or client count)
E. License and Regulatory Documents

Upload regulatory credentials, business license, ESG certifications.
AI or staff validation against public registries.
Optional “Verified Status” Program for both user and company

Show a blue badge for:
Verified portfolio
Reference checks passed
Certification + deal activity confirmed
